## Ahlan! 👋

**Arabyy** is a structured Modern Standard Arabic (Fusha) course for English speakers. Short, focused lessons stack from the basics upward — similar to how Duolingo builds skills, but with full explanations, tables, and practice.

Your goals: **build sentences**, grow **practical vocabulary**, and understand **spoken Arabic** (news, talks, formal conversation).

<Callout title="New here? Start with Unit 1" type="info">
  Open [Unit 1 Lesson 1: Arabic Letters Review](/notes/phase-1-getting-started/unit-01-script-and-sounds/01-arabic-letters-review) and work through each unit in order. Every lesson teaches one skill and builds on what came before.
</Callout>

## Course structure

| Phase | Units | Focus |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Phase 1** | 1 to 4 | Script, greetings, simple sentences, questions |
| **Phase 2** | 5 to 8 | People, routines, food, travel |
| **Phase 3** | 9 to 12 | Past and future, conjunctions, comparatives |
| **Phase 4** | 13 to 16 | Conversations, news, listening, full integration |

**Pacing:** One unit per week at steady pace ≈ 16 weeks (~4 months).
