## Skill Summary

<P>After Unit 1, you should be able to:</P>

<OL>
  <LI><Strong>Lesson 1:</Strong> Name and recognize all 28 Arabic letters in isolated form and spot lookalike letters by their dots.</LI>
  <LI><Strong>Lesson 2:</Strong> Read letters with fatha, kasra, damma, sukun, and shadda.</LI>
  <LI><Strong>Lesson 3:</Strong> Follow connected letter chains and tell initial, medial, and final shapes apart.</LI>
  <LI><Strong>Lesson 4:</Strong> Read common two and three letter words aloud with full vowels.</LI>
  <LI><Strong>Lesson 5:</Strong> Hear and say the difference between commonly confused sound pairs.</LI>
</OL>
